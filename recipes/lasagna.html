Lasagna

Description

The Best Classic Lasagna Recipe – Homemade lasagna full of pasta, ricotta, a homemade meat sauce, and tons of cheese. This is the world’s best lasagna recipe!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Salt
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 (15-ounce) package whole milk ricotta
  • 3 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella, divided
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and lighlt oil a baking dish or similar.
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Cook the ground beef and the onion. Add salt and pepper. when beef has browned Stir in tomatoes and italian seasoning until well combined
  4. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella and egg; set aside
  5. Spread 1 cup tomato mixture onto the bottom of a 9×13 baking dish; top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat with a second layer. Top with remaining noodles, tomato mixture, 1 cup mozzarella cheese and Parmesan.
  7. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots.
  8. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired.
